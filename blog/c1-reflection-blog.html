Reflections

07.Feb.2018

On Monday i started with an orentation call using zoom. This was basically like a welcome/introduction of whats going to happen, what to expect,
and a chance to meet everyone and ask any questions/queries we may have. That afternoon i started working on the first sprint

Enspiral Dev Academy has a different view towards education. I personaly think the old education system is super outdated for the world today.
I think EDA shares similar views aswell, and are gearing up for a more future friendly way towards learning. The style of learning at EDA seems more
mentorship based. More as guides to help you self discover and take ownership of your learning, rather that regurgitation of knowledge.
The approach to other skills EDA refers to as 'soft skiils' or 'engineering empathy' is also something I love and think is nessacary. I think
that its important to also emphasize on teach skills that machines cannot learn (for now), and I love how a big part of EDA is about co operating
and being able to interact, team up, and work with humans. Human skills. Its so underrated! teaching how to think/act not what to think/act, (if that makes sense).

Im Really looking forward to engaging and becoming apart of the Tech world. I am abit nervous when I think about how much I am going to do, but
at the same time so excited and can't wait to see myself and everyone in the cohort how far we've all come at the end :)
So i guess this is me in week 1. Im almost finished the first sprint. Heres to one step closer to my visions!
