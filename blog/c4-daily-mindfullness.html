Daily Mindfulness

  • What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?
    • I havn't done this formally but I do try to consciously do this in everyday life. It feels like you are very present and in control of your feeling and reactions. You observe take in and think about it alot better when you give them full attention without butting in or talking or just wildly reacting to something they say. I find when you just listern, then when you make it clear when its your time to speak the person almost mimick you and does the same. Like they're sub conscious is secretly picking up on your awesome habits and how you are reacting in the situation and mimicks you and adjusts to match. You feel alot more powerfull and respected, authority. It seems as if when you do have something to say, its alot more powerful and depth to it and is heard.
  • Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?
    • I like the mindful walking, and just mindful living basically. I am going to try and every now and then each day take some time and just consciously be there present with what Im doing and put everything else to the back of the mind or the peripherals.
